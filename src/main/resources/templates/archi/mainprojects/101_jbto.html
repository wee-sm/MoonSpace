Narakium Jungbu Tax Office new construction project
This project was my first design development and construction project. I performed 3D modeling, intersection checking, drawing, documents preparing, etc as assistant architecture.
As an architect assistant, I participated in 160K GSF new construction project. This project was following the strict standards related to structure and environment because it is a government tax office. Through the finishing of this project, I could understand the meaning of various documents for construction.
The site is located on the border between the nature of mountain Namsan and the flow from Seoul city center and is placed in the junction where various road axes passed. Analyzing the population flow on the axes moving from Chungmuro to Myeongdong had to be considered prior to the design.
As the Jungbu tax office is in charge of Myeongdong and Dongdaemun markets, it should embrace the representativeness of the region and the publicness for the citizens. As a means to generate the symbolic feature, the tax office was designed using 'varied facets' which not only represent the various axes but also reflect the dynamic characteristics of the surrounding area, Myeongdong and Dongdaemun. Also, in order to provide publicness to the majority, a spacious open space was designed in front of the office to provide a resting place to the pedestrians.

Our team consisted of 5 manpower Including myself, and I was an assistant designer from the Design Development to the Construction Administration. Followings are the main duties I carried out:

  • Designed plans and estimated the cost of the temporary tax office building which will substitute the original Jungbu tax office during the construction.
  • Checked the interference between the structure and the facilities.
  • Created working drawings used in the construction, such as location drawings, assemblydrawings and component drawings.
  • Produces final construction documentation.
  • Revised documents for permission, accreditation and value engineering(following intensified architectural standards for a public institution)

During this public office project, we performed designs based on higher architectural standard and produced well-made full packaged output. As a result, this project is acknowledged as one of the finest projects in the company.
