Company Building
Office Building Remodeling Project
Seoul, South Korea
 As the 1st generation of an e-commerce company, Dholic is now expanding its services to the world. This building will be the center of the company, from sales to branding. That is why the project symbolically shows the challenges, creatives and innovations of leading companies through facades. It also applied an eco-friendly building plan to improve work efficiency in office space.
The project won the grand prize for the 2018 Korean Remodeling Building.
Facade Design
There are two different facade concepts of upper and lower parts. The lower section has a podium space with columns where pedestrians can stay and observe the products. And at the upper section, a combination of existing structural grids and new tilted windows shows the concept of the fabric composition. In the old urban contexts, this building can represent the creativity and individuality of the company.
Detail Development
The target of this remodeling project was a building that has been over 30 years old. Therefore, the first thing that had to consider was structural issues that not enough for nowadays standards. Therefore this project started with an analysis of the weakness of building performance such as insulation, HVAC, emergency alarms, etc, in addition to the structure.
Basically, the design was planned to maintain existing frameworks and minimize the structural burden. And overall facilities were repaired or exchanged to meet the current standards.
Facade Detail
Tilted windows and reinforcement details
On the irregular existing structure, detailed cross section planning was required to plan regular facade grids and slanted windows. So we measured the structural status of all floors, and drew detailed drawings considering structural reinforcement.
Finally, the overall remodeling was carried out by finishing the building's elevation with limeston and clear glass.
